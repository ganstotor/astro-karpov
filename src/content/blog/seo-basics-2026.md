# SEO Basics in 2026

SEO has evolved far beyond keywords.

## Content Quality

Google prioritizes:

- Helpful content
- Expertise
- Original information

AI-generated content must still provide value.

## Technical SEO

Important factors include:

- Fast loading speed
- Mobile optimization
- Proper metadata
- Structured data

## Internal Linking

Link related articles together to improve crawlability.

Example:

- Guides
- Tutorials
- Case studies

## User Experience

Google measures how users interact with your site.

Better UX = Better rankings.
