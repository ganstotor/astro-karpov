# Content Strategy for Startups

Content is leverage.

## Step 1: Define Your Niche

Don't write about everything.

Example:

- Not "Marketing"
- But "SEO for Legal Tech Startups"

Specific wins.

## Step 2: Create Topic Clusters

Main topic:

- Legal Marketing

Supporting articles:

- SEO for law firms
- Paid ads for lawyers
- Email funnels for attorneys

Google loves depth.

## Step 3: Publish Consistently

Consistency > volume.

1–2 high-quality posts per week is enough.

## Step 4: Repurpose Content

Turn blog posts into:

- LinkedIn posts
- Twitter threads
- Newsletter content
- YouTube scripts

Multiply reach.

---

Content compounds over time.  
Start early.
